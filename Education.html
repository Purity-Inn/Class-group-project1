Magufuli’s Education

John Pombe Magufuli, the fifth president of Tanzania had an impressive Educational journey

  • Magufuli began his education at Chato Primary School from 1967 to 1974. He then attended Katoke Seminary in Biharamulo for secondary education from 1975 to 1977. Later, he relocated to Lake Secondary School, where he graduated in 1978. Advanced Level Studies: Magufuli pursued his Advanced level studies at Mkwawa High School in 1979 and successfully graduated in 1981.

  • He continued his academic journey at the University of Dar es Salaam: Bachelor of Science in Education (Chemistry and Mathematics) in 1988. Masters degree in Chemistry in 1994. Doctorate in Chemistry in 200912.

Magufuli’s educational background reflects his commitment to learning and his diverse interests, from teaching to industrial chemistry.
